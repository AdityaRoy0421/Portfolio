About Me

Aditya Raj Chaudhary

3D Artist & Blender Enthusiast

Hello! I'm Aditya aka Roy, a passionate 3D artist enthusiast working in Blender and a 3rd Year student at IISER Pune. Although I have not done a formal course in 3d Art or animation, I am building and learning on my own. With roughly 4 years of experience, I've worked on a diverse range of projects from character modelling and animation to architectural visualization and game assets. I thrive on bringing creative visions to life and continuously exploring new techniques in the ever-evolving world of 3D. I also work on scientific models and bring concepts to life.

My goal is to create compelling and high-quality 3D content that tells a story and captivates the audience. Contact me for any query or collaboration.
